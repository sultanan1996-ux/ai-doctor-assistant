## Scenario: Chest Pain

### Purpose
Assist doctors in evaluating chest pain safely and systematically.
This tool is assistive, not diagnostic.

### Inputs
- Age
- Sex
- Nature of pain
- Duration
- Radiation
- Associated symptoms
- Risk factors

### Differential Diagnosis
- Acute coronary syndrome
- Musculoskeletal pain
- GERD
- Pulmonary embolism
- Aortic dissection

### Red Flags
- Crushing chest pain
- Radiation to arm or jaw
- Syncope
- Hypotension

### Recommended Initial Actions
- ECG
- Troponin
- Chest X-ray
- Vital signs

### Safety Notice
Clinical decision support only.
Final decisions must be made by a licensed physician.
